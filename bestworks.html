Junior Year

The Alice in Wonderland play during my Junior year was a great accomplishment and experience for me. I played the role of the King of Hearts. The original student who was assigned this role never showed up to practices.

read more...

The day before the first performance, I happened to be in the same room and was asked to fill in the role temporarily. I went up with my script and actually did very well. Ms. Begay ended up asking me to take over the role! Being me I said yes, however, I didn't have much time to memorize my lines and practice. So I memorized them two hours before the play the next day after school! Participating in the play further enhanced my "on-the-spot" creative ability. It taught me to be expressive in my speech. During the time I was filling in for the King, I had to read from the script, and make the character interesting using my improv skills. I was forced to think quickly to solve a problem and in the end, I was awarded with the role of the King of Hearts.
