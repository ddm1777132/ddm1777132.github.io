Extracurricular Activities

Activities

Cantabile

Cantabile is an advance choir ensemble that I have been a part of since my freshman year. It has helped me grow my musical talent and strengthen my relationship with the Lord. the Cantabile has performed at many events and each one I have seen God work through our music to touch peoples' hearts.

Track & Field

Track has been my most favorite sport. Even throughout all the injuries I've had, I have always enjoyed running. Running track has taught me about how intricately designed our bodies are. God knew what He was doing when He made us. The fact the our bodies are so well designed by God has helped me to appreciate more, the fact that I am made in the image of God.

Youth Group

Youth Group has been one of the places where I can go to talk to God. I am surrounded by wonderful and caring people who are always there for me if I need them. Youth Group is one of the places that I feel closest to God. The atmosphere during worship is sincere and those who are present actually want to be there.
